Fried Chicken

Fried chicken, also known as Southern fried chicken, is a dish consisting of chicken pieces that have been coated with seasoned flour or batter and pan-fried, deep fried, pressure fried, or air fried. The breading adds a crisp coating or crust to the exterior of the chicken while retaining juices in the meat. Broiler chickens are most commonly used.

    Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Garlic salt
  • Paprika
  • Pepper
  • Poultry seasoning
  • large eggs
  • water
  • salt
  • broiler/frier chickens
  • Oil

    Steps

  1. Combine flour, garlic salt, paprika, pepper and poultry seasoning
  2. Beat eggs and water in another shallow dish
  3. Add salt and flour and pepper to the dish
  4. Dip chicken in egg mixture then place in flour mixture
  5. Fry chicken in oil
  6. Drain on paper towels
