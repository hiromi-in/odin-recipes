Sushi

Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, accompanied by a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, often raw, and vegetables.

    Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Rice
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Soy sauce

    Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Season the rice
  3. Prepare the topping
  4. Shape the rice
  5. Assemble
  6. With or without nori seaweed
